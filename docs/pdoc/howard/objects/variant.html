def justify_indel(start, end, indel, seq, justify)

Justify an indel to the left or right along a sequence 'seq'.

start, end: 0-based, end-exclusive coordinates of 'indel' within the sequence 'seq'. Inserts denote the insertion point using start=end and deletions indicate the deleted region with (start,end). indel: indel sequence, can be insertion or deletion. seq: a larger sequence containing the indel. Can be a fragment from the genome. justify: Which direction to justify the indel ('left', 'right').
